FSAE Frame Jig

Precision Alignment & Assembly System

Objectives

  • Design a frame jig system for the Formula SAE vehicle for SFU Team Phantom.
  • Ensure precise alignment and assembly of the car frame components.
  • Enhance structural integrity and accuracy of the frame.

Outcomes & Contributions

  • Developed a frame jig system using wooden boards and 3D printed donuts for angle alignments.
  • Achieved near-perfect frame assembly in physical model.
  • Learned about the different tolerances of different FDM printers and incorporating different print calibrations into designing the parts.

Technical Skills

  • Additive Manufacturing
  • GD&T (Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing)
  • Laser Cutting
  • Solidworks CAD
  • Tolerance Analysis
