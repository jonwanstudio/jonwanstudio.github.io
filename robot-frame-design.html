Robot Frame Design

Structural Design & Analysis

Objectives

  • Needs to withstand up to 11 lbf without breaking or significant deflection.
  • Maintain a lightweight design to enhance efficiency.
  • Remain cost-effective in material and production.
  • Facilitate easy assembly and disassembly.

Outcomes & Contributions

  • Selected Aluminum Frame with Cube Shape as the optimal design choice due to symmetrical weight distribution.
  • The frame is made out of 2020 aluminum extrusions from McMaster-Carr.
  • Leveraged aluminum for its machinability and corrosion resistance.
  • Confirmed frame's load-bearing capacity and structural integrity through FEA.

Technical Skills

  • Design for Manufacturing (DFM)
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • Solidworks CAD
  • Material Selection
  • Structural Analysis
