About Me

"I believe in creating engineering solutions that are not only precise and reliable, but also efficient, intuitive, and impactful in real-world applications."

I thrive on solving complex engineering challenges, from automating validation workflows and prototyping electromechanical systems to leading design optimization in Formula SAE.

When I’m not engineering or testing hardware, you’ll find me designing vehicle components, optimizing 3D prints, or experimenting with automation and embedded systems.
