About Me

Hello! I’m Jonathan Wan, a mechatronic systems engineer with 3+ years of hands-on experience in hardware validation, product design, and system integration across automotive, imaging, and medical technologies. I thrive on solving complex engineering challenges, from automating validation workflows and prototyping electromechanical systems to leading design optimization in Formula SAE. My passion lies at the intersection of innovation and reliability, creating solutions that bridge mechanical precision with intelligent systems to drive the future of mobility and connected technology.

When I’m not engineering or testing hardware, you’ll find me designing vehicle components, optimizing 3D prints, or experimenting with automation and embedded systems.
